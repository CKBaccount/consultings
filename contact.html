Our campus is within walking distance to many amenities and downtown destinations including The Forks, Winnipeg Square and SHED (Sports, Hospitality and Entertainment District). Students can take Health and Community Services hybrid programs with lab classes as well as online programs in Business and Technology.

Students will have access to a number of in-demand programs and services, delivered in learning classrooms and laboratories.  The campus is on a major transit route.
